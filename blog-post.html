Popular Places To Visit In Egypt

by Afolabi Peters

I know you have been wondering where you could go to in Egypt. Well if you are lover of history, you have come to the right place! With so many historical artifacts, you will never get tired or bored of exploring and learning the stories behind these places i have for you.

For starters, we have the Pyramids of Giza which is known as one of the world’s most recognizable landmarks. It is a place with ancient history that will leave you in awe.

If you have heard about Egypt and the biblical occurrences that happened there, you must have heard of the Nile, a perfect place to take a cruise for the boat lovers and also have a history moment.

Then we have Abu Simbel, again with the history we all know temples hold a lot of that and Abu Simbel is not lacking!

We also have the Islamic Cairo which is full of mosques and Islamic schools of learning. If you want to see the architecture of old Islamic empires, then Islamic Cairo is the place to go.

Another mind thrilling place is the Monastery of St. Anthony which is an active monastery that has monks as residents and of course is a hidden sight which is a norm to monasteries by nothern mountains of the Red Sea coast.

Other places that are staples in Egypt include South Sinai’s Beach, Saqqara, Alexandria ( Founded by Alexander the Great and home of Cleopatra ), and Temple of Hathor. You are bound to have a pleasant and educational stay in Egypt.
